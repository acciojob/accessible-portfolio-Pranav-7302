Biography

Yogi Adityanath

Yogi Adityanath, born Ajay Mohan Singh Bisht on June 5, 1972, is an Indian Hindu monk and politician who has served as the 22nd and current Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh since March 2017. His remarkable transformation from a shy and introverted boy to a powerful politician is a testament to his dedication, hard work, and spiritual upbringing.
